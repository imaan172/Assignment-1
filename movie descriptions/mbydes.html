Me Before You

Directed by Thea Sharrock

Released on 3 June 2016
Starring: Emilia Clarke and Sam Claflin

Movie Description

"Me Before You" is a romantic drama film based on the novel of the same name by Jojo Moyes.

"Me Before You" tells the story of Louisa "Lou" Clark, a quirky and cheerful young woman who becomes the caregiver for Will Traynor, a wealthy and successful businessman who becomes paralyzed after a tragic accident. Initially, Will is bitter and reclusive due to his disability, and Lou struggles to connect with him.

As their relationship develops, Lou and Will form a deep bond. Lou introduces Will to the simple pleasures of life, and he, in turn, encourages her to broaden her horizons. Despite their differences, they fall in love.

However, a significant part of the movie revolves around the ethical and emotional complexities surrounding Will's desire to end his life through assisted suicide. He believes he can never have the life he once had and wants to end his suffering. Lou is torn between her love for Will and her respect for his wishes.

The film explores themes of love, disability, and the right to choose one's own destiny. It's a heartwarming and heartbreaking story of two people who find love in unexpected places but must ultimately make difficult decisions about their future.
